**BITS Pilani (Pilani Campus), Rajasthan** – the flagship campus of India's premier private engineering university, established in 1964 on a 328-acre residential campus in Pilani town, Rajasthan.  As the original "BITS," it pioneered India's private engineering excellence with its unique Practice School system, flexible dual degrees, and unmatched alumni network powering global tech giants.[1][2][3]

***

## Overview

BITS Pilani's Pilani campus remains the heart of the BITS ecosystem, mentoring Goa and Hyderabad while maintaining its legacy as India's first private deemed university focused on science and technology.  Located 200 km west of Delhi in the serene Shekhawati region, the campus houses 4,000+ students across UG, PG, and PhD programs in a self-contained township with labs, hostels, sports complexes, and research centers.[2][3][1]
