- **Title**: 24pt Bold (white on colored background)

- **Footer**: 8pt Normal (page numbers)

### Spacing
- **Line Height**: 6mm (good readability)
- **Section Spacing**: 12mm (visual separation)
- **Paragraph Spacing**: 4mm (breathing room)
- **Header Bottom Spacing**: 6mm (separates from content)
- **Margins**: 18mm left/right, 20mm top/bottom
